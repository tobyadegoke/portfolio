Cloud 9 -Immerse Yourself, Feel The Rhythm

Challenge

Cloud 9 is an audio experience brand seeking to elevate the experience of sounds and rhythm for everyone around the world. Using cutting-edge immersion technology combined with custom design made to suit a wide range of users, Cloud 9 produces audio enhancing experience across various applications. As Cloud 9 decided to go into the market, they needed a visual identity that clearly communicated their differentiation and premium product.

Process

To help gain insight on how to best communicate Cloud 9’s differentitation, I took a deep-dive into the world of Cloud 9 through discovery sessions and interviews. Cloud 9 uses the latest immersive technologies to create a surreal audio experience helping them feel sounds and rhythm in an entirely new way, almost like a dream sequence.

Solution

I built a design system built around the concept of simplicity and surreal experience. This graphics system was used across various touchpoints to tell brand stories about various products and ideas. The sharp edges of the Cloud 9 logo gives it a geometric feel and a touch of modernity.

Headset Design

It was essential that customers understood the benefits of the products that Cloud 9 offers so I created messaging that spoke directly to customers without too much complexity and the usual industry jargon.

Cloud 9 now has a cohesive brand to help differentiate them from their competitors. With the aspiration to grow beyond it’s current offering, my work offers Cloud 9 to grow while maintaining concistency. It was an amazing experience working on this project and I can’t wait to see what’s next for Cloud 9.
