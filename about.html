About Me

My name is Oluwatobi Adegoke (Tobi) and I am primarily a Graphics, Motion and Web Designer. I also have knowledge & skills across several other fields including UI Design, Illustration, Video Editing, 3d Design and Frontend Development.

I love creating amazing visual experiences and I'm passionate about solving problems through research-driven processes that translate to real world applications with humans as the focus.

Currently, I work as the Creative Lead at Ngage Media Global. Involved in each phase of the design process from discovery, ideation, visual design strategy to user testing and scaling.

I am a general tech enthusiast always researching about the latest technologies in my spare time when I’m not playing games, watching anime, listening to music or creating music.

I am currently based in Abuja, Nigeria working with people and businesses all over the world.
