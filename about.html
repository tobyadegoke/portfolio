About Me

My name is Oluwatobi Adegoke (Tobi) and I am primarily a Graphics, Motion and Web Designer. I also have knowledge & skills across several other fields including UI Design, Illustration, Video Editing, 3d Design and Frontend Development.

I love creating amazing visual experiences and I'm passionate about solving problems. I am constantly fueled by my aspiration to leverage design as a tool for making social impact and driving a better future

Having worked over the past 5 years as a Creative Lead and Digital Marketing Strategist in various organizations and industries I have developed my problem solving skills through various challenges and obstacles.

I am a general tech enthusiast, always researching about the latest technologies in my spare time when I’m not playing games, listening to music (or trying to create music), dancing alone in front of my mirror.

I currently head design efforts at Goke Studios, a design and animation studio and also work as designer at Nitax Technologies Limited and DeckDash Limited. I am currently based in Abuja, Nigeria working with people and businesses all over the world.

I’m always open for a chat, get in touch to find out how I can help advance your business to the next phase...
